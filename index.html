Neural Network Abstraction Explorer

A neural network is an abstract computer model inspired by the brain. The human brain is estimated to have 10^11 neurons and 10^15 links. This simulation is a tiny scaled abstraction for learning, not a literal rendering of those full biological numbers.

  • Click near a neuron to stimulate it and watch activity propagate.
  • Press 1/2/3 to switch mode: perception, cognition, learning.
  • Press O to toggle overlays, Space to pause/resume, R to reset.
  • Press H to show/hide control help in the canvas panel.
